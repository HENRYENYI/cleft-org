About Our Foundation

Dedicated to transforming lives through comprehensive healthcare

Our Mission

The Foundation for Better Health Initiatives (fbhint.org) is committed to providing comprehensive medical support for children with cleft conditions and elderly individuals in underserved communities. We believe that quality healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege.

Our Vision

To create a world where every child born with a cleft condition receives timely, quality treatment, and where elderly individuals have access to dignified healthcare services regardless of their economic circumstances.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We approach every patient with empathy and understanding
  • Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in medical care
  • Accessibility: We ensure healthcare reaches those who need it most
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and accountability

Our History

2018

Foundation Established

fbhint.org was founded by a group of medical professionals passionate about addressing healthcare gaps in underserved communities.

2019

First Medical Camp

Organized our first medical camp, providing free cleft surgeries to 25 children in rural areas.

2020

Elderly Care Program Launch

Expanded our services to include comprehensive elderly care programs in partnership with local healthcare providers.

2021

Mobile Clinic Initiative

Launched mobile clinics to reach remote communities, significantly increasing our service coverage.

2023

500th Surgery Milestone

Celebrated performing our 500th cleft surgery, marking a significant milestone in our mission.
