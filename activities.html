December 15, 2023 Medical Camps

Successful Medical Camp Reaches 150 Patients in Rural Community

Our latest medical camp in the remote village of Maplewood brought essential healthcare services to over 150 patients, including 25 children requiring cleft surgery consultations. The three-day camp was our most successful outreach program to date.

Working alongside local healthcare providers, our team of 12 medical professionals provided free consultations, medications, and scheduled follow-up surgeries for children with cleft conditions. The camp also included health education sessions for families and community members.

November 28, 2023 Elderly Care

New Elderly Care Program Launches in Three Communities

We're excited to announce the launch of our comprehensive elderly care program, now serving three rural communities with regular health check-ups, medication management, and social support services.

The program includes monthly visits from our mobile clinic team, providing essential healthcare services to elderly residents who have limited access to medical facilities.

November 10, 2023 Training

Healthcare Worker Training Program Graduates 30 Professionals

Our intensive training program has successfully graduated 30 healthcare workers, equipping them with specialized skills in cleft care and elderly health management. Watch the highlights from our graduation ceremony.

The six-month program covered surgical techniques, post-operative care, family counseling, and community health education. These newly trained professionals will serve in rural healthcare facilities across the region.

October 22, 2023 Success Stories

Little Sarah's Journey: From Cleft to Confident Smile

Meet Sarah, a 6-year-old whose life was transformed through our cleft surgery program. Her journey from initial consultation to full recovery showcases the impact of comprehensive cleft care.

Sarah's surgery was performed by our team of specialists, followed by months of speech therapy and family support. Today, she attends school with confidence and dreams of becoming a teacher.
